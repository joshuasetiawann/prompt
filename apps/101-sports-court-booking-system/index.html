From search to confirmed

Book a court in five clear steps

Every screen is purpose-built — from the venue catalog to the availability board, checkout, and recurring setup.

1

Browse the catalog

Filter venues by sport, location, and live availability to find an open court near you.

2

Pick a slot on the board

Tap a peak or off-peak cell on the per-court time grid for your chosen date.

3

Checkout at your rate

Your member or casual rate applies automatically, then mock payment confirms the booking.

4

Set it to repeat

Turn any slot into a weekly recurring booking that skips maintenance and tournament blocks.

5

Manage from one account

Review upcoming and past bookings, download receipts, and reschedule within the cutoff window.
