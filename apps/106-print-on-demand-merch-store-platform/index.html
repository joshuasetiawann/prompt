Everything in the box

From blank product to printed parcel, in one workflow.

InkRoute ships the full print-on-demand stack — a live designer, variant-aware catalog, snapshotting checkout, partner routing, and owner analytics — so you launch a store instead of stitching tools together.

🎨

Live mockup designer

Upload artwork and place it on any blank product with a real-time preview that updates as you scale, move, and recolor.

👕

Variant-aware catalog

Blank products carry size and color variants plus defined print areas, so designs always land where they actually print.

💾

Saved designs

Keep custom designs in your library and reuse the same artwork across products and repeat orders without redoing the work.

🛒

Snapshotting checkout

A mock-payment cart locks in each item's artwork, placement, and variant so what's ordered is exactly what gets printed.

🔀

Automatic partner routing

Every paid order item auto-routes to a print partner that supports its product type and shipping region — no manual hand-off.

📦

Fulfillment tracking

Follow each item from received to in-production to shipped, with tracking numbers surfaced on the customer's order page.

🗂️

Print-partner queue

Partners accept assigned jobs and push in-production and shipped status updates from a dedicated fulfillment queue.

🏷️

Markup pricing

Retail price is built from each product's base cost, print options, and the creator's markup — transparent on every item.

📊

Owner reports

Track sales, average fulfillment time, and partner performance so you know what's selling and who's shipping fastest.

Who it's for

Four roles, each with the screens they need.

InkRoute scopes every view to its role — shoppers design, owners run the store, partners fulfill, and admins keep the platform healthy.

🧑‍🎨

Shopper Designs & buys

Creates custom products on the live mockup designer, saves favorite designs, and places orders.

  • Design on a real-time mockup canvas
  • Reuse saved designs across products
  • Track orders and tracking numbers
🏪

Store Owner Runs the store

Manages the blank catalog, designs, and pricing, and keeps an eye on every order moving through fulfillment.

  • Manage blank products and print areas
  • Set base cost, print options, and markup
  • Review sales and fulfillment reports
🖨️

Print Partner Fulfills & ships

Receives routed orders in a dedicated queue, accepts jobs, and pushes status from in-production to shipped.

  • Work an assigned fulfillment queue
  • Accept, produce, and mark shipped
  • Attach tracking numbers to items
🛡️

Admin Oversees platform

Manages stores and print partners, watches order routing, and reviews platform-wide revenue reports.

  • Manage stores and print partners
  • Audit order routing decisions
  • Review platform revenue reports
