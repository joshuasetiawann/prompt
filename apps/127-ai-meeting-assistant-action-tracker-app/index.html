What ScribeFlow does

From raw recording to accountable follow-through.

Nine capabilities that take a meeting from audio to a tracked, owned, searchable record — without anyone typing notes.

🎙️

Upload & record

Drop in an audio file or capture live in the browser. Every meeting enters a processing queue with a visible status from queued to ready.

📝

Speaker-labeled transcripts

AI transcription produces a timestamped, speaker-attributed transcript you can read, scan, and jump through moment by moment.

✨

Decision-ready summaries

A structured summary distills the overview, the decisions made, and the discussion highlights — no more scrubbing the recording.

✅

Auto-extracted action items

ScribeFlow pulls out the to-dos and suggests an owner and a due date for each, so nothing said out loud gets forgotten.

🔄

Assign & track to done

Hand items to teammates and move them through open, in progress, and done — visible on a board and in each person's task list.

⏱️

Linked to the source moment

Every summary point and action item references the exact transcript timestamp it came from, so context is one click away.

🔍

Search across everything

Full-text search spans transcripts, summaries, and action items in your workspace — find that one commitment in seconds.

🔒

Per-workspace isolation

Data is scoped per workspace with member roles, so each team only ever sees its own meetings, notes, and tasks.

🔔

Overdue & upcoming alerts

Notifications flag items that are coming due or already overdue, with a mock email log so the full flow runs without a provider.
