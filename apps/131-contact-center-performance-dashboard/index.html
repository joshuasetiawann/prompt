The platform

Everything a support floor measures, in one operational view.

From the live queue to the post-contact survey, PulseDesk computes the metrics that matter and turns threshold breaches into action — color-coded, scoped to your role, and exportable.

📡

Live queue board

Auto-refreshing view of queue volume, longest wait, and available agents across every voice and chat queue.

📊

Core KPI engine

Average handle time, first-contact resolution, and SLA compliance computed continuously and recomputed on every filter.

🎯

Color-coded scorecards

Per-agent and per-team scorecards measured against targets, with on-target, watch, and breaching status at a glance.

🕐

Shift comparison

Shift-based aggregation that lets you put morning, swing, and overnight scorecards side by side to spot coverage gaps.

⭐

CSAT rollup

Capture post-contact survey scores and roll them up into agent and team averages automatically — no spreadsheet stitching.

🚩

Automatic flagging

Agents who breach a metric threshold are raised into the coaching queue the moment the numbers cross the line.

📝

Coaching workflow

Open a review straight from a flag, log a summary and action items, set a follow-up date, and track it to closure.

🔎

SLA breach drill-down

Click any SLA breach to see the exact contacts that missed the threshold — the wait, the handle time, the agent.

📤

CSV export

Export scorecards and SLA reports to CSV for QBRs, payroll calibration, and workforce planning in seconds.
