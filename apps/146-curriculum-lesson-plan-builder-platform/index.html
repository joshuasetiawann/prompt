How PlanForge works

Four screens take a plan from blank page to approved.

Each step maps to a real page in the app — from the standards browser to the review queue — so nothing falls through the cracks between planning and sign-off.

1

Browse the standards library

Filter standards by subject and grade level, then pull the codes your unit needs to cover.

2

Build & sequence the unit

Add lessons in order from the objective and resource library; covered standards roll up as you go.

3

Check the term coverage map

See the standards-vs-units matrix, spot uncovered standards as gaps, and fix them before submitting.

4

Route through review

Submit to your department head, who approves or requests changes — all tracked in the review queue.
