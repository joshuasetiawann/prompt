2 Pay run review

Check the math before anyone signs off

See gross, total deductions, and net for every employee in one register, with run totals at the bottom. Tax brackets and statutory deductions are applied line by line, so the net is the net.

  • Per-employee gross-to-net with itemized lines
  • Run-level totals for gross, deductions, and net
  • Add off-cycle bonuses or one-time deductions inline
3 Approval queue

Route the run, hold the line on sign-off

Approvers see every run awaiting a decision with its totals in plain view. A run can't be marked paid until it's approved — the gate is enforced in the workflow, not just the UI.

  • Approve or reject with run totals in context
  • Paid state is blocked until approval is recorded
  • Audit trail of who approved each run and when
